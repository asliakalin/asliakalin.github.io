CS70: Discrete Mathematics & Probability Theory

I've taught this class for 6 semesters; 4 semesters as a TA; and 1 semester as a TA + Head of Content.

  • Logic, Propositions and Mathematical Induction: proof techniques, recursion, the stable marriage problem and undecidability.
  • Arithmetic Algorithms: modular arithmetic, GCDs, simple finite fields, primality testing, the RSA cryptosystem and cryptography.
  • Polynomials and their Applications: error-correcting codes, interpolation and secret sharing.
  • Diagonalization, Self-Reference and Uncomputability: NP Problems, proofs and reduction.
  • Probability and Probabilistic Algorithms: random variables, independence, law of large numbers, load balancing, probabilistic constructions, existence arguments, conditional probability, Bayesian inference, intro to continuous probability.

    • CS169: Software Engineering

    I've taught this class for 2 semesters as a TA, 1 as a TA + Head of Content.

  • Learn: Ruby on Rails, Javascript, jQuery, SQLite, Pivotal, CodeClimate, Heroku, Cucumber, Capybara and Git
  • Agile, Saas & SOA: HTTP communication, model-view-controller, microservices, client-server
  • Requirements, Specifications & Design: Unified Modeling Language (UML), Design Matterns
  • Testing & Debugging: General principles, regression testing, integratio testing, test generation, delta debugging
  • Runtime Monitoring: Purify, Cooperative Bug Isolation, Diduce, Eraser (data race debugging)
  • Software Quality: Software Security, Verification, Refactoring, Software Reuse
  • Programming with Exceptions: Filters and validations
